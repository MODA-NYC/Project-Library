MODA Open Source Analytics Library

MODA partners with agencies to create, test, and improve analytic models that deliver measurable value to City services. These partnerships allow for analytic insight to empower on-the-ground expertise.

Free Lunch For All

One in every 67 Americans under the age of 18 is a public school student in New York City. MODA partnered with the Department of Education to help its Office of School Support Services deliver free lunch to them all.

Legionnaires' Disease Response

MODA assisted in a citywide response effort after an outbreak of Legionnaires' Disease.

Fighting Source of Income Discrimination

The NYC Commission on Human Rights partnered with MODA to assist in identifying sites of potential income discrimination.
